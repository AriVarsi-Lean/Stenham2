Stenham Private School

Background

Stenham Private School is a South African registered Independent Private Primary School, that prescribes and implements curriculum for physical, mental, emotional, intellectual and spiritual progress for achieving multifaceted development and growth of the younger generation. The School provides an environment for the development of quality leadership, team spirit, sportsmanship, self-esteem and other social attributes to prepare children to be future leaders to ensure Continuity and Growth as a Nation. The School provides a happy, caring and safe environment for your child, through working hard to ensure that our learners experience of school is a positive one and one which encourages a love of learning and self-discipline from the start.

Mission

The mission of stenham Private School is to increase the value of the School, and the value of our global portfolio of diversified education services by exceeding our clients expectations and achieving market leadership while operating in excellence in every segment of the School. We inspire, prepare and empower the South African Nations’ young generation to succeed in a rapidly ever changing African economy. This made possible by following the Stenham School Core values.

Our Goals

    • To prescribe and implement curriculum for physical, mental, emotional, intellectual and spiritual progress for achieving multifaceted development and growth of the young generation.
    • To provide an environment for the development of quality leadership, team spirit, sportsmanship, self-esteem and other social attributes to prepare children for facing real life challenges.
    • To shape global minds in a young South African Soul.
    • To provide access to world-class infrastructure and a practical curriculum that promotes all-round development of the child with proper values.
    • To enter into a strategic alliance with the world class education institutions and universities for continuous renewal of state-of-art infrastructure to expose children in the international arena of school education.

Our Values

    • Service Excellence.
    • Listening to our clients.
    • Establishing a firm relationship with our clients
    • Ensuring that our products remain globally competitive.
    • Institution and parents' Team work.
    • Responding to our market segment’s needs.
