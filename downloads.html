The Institution

Stenham Private School is a fully registered and independent private primary school, that prescribes and implements a curriculum for physical, mental, emotional, intellectual and spiritual progress for achieving multi-faceted development and growth of the younger generation. The school provides an environment for the development of quality leadership, team spirit, sportsmanship, self-esteem and other social attributes to prepare children to be future leaders to ensure continuity and growth as a nation. The School provides a happy, caring and safe environment for your child by working hard to ensure that our learners experience of school is a positive one which fosters a love of learning and self-discipline from the start.
